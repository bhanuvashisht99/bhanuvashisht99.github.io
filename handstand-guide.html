Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be strong to start?
Basic push-up strength is helpful, but this guide focuses on technique and positioning first. Many people fail at handstands due to poor alignment, not lack of strength. The progressive system builds strength as you progress.
How long until I can hold a freestanding handstand?
This varies greatly based on your starting point and practice consistency. The guide gives you a structured path - follow the progressions and don't skip steps. Focus on quality practice over rushing to freestanding.
Do I need any equipment?
Just a wall and floor space. Yoga blocks can be helpful but aren't required. The focus is on understanding mechanics and consistent practice.

Your satisfaction is our priority

Overview

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. This policy outlines our cancellation and refund terms for both digital products and online training services.

Digital Products (PDF Guides)

⚠️ No Refunds on Digital Products

Due to the nature of digital products, all sales of PDF guides are final and non-refundable once the download link has been sent to your email.

Why No Refunds?

  • Digital products are delivered instantly and cannot be "returned"
  • Once downloaded, the content cannot be un-accessed
  • To prevent fraud and unauthorized sharing of content

Exceptions for Digital Products

Refunds for digital products will ONLY be considered in the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If the download link is invalid and we cannot provide a working link within 48 hours
  • Duplicate Purchase: If you accidentally purchased the same product twice
  • Payment Error: If you were charged but did not receive the product and we cannot deliver it

We understand emergencies happen. In case of genuine emergencies (medical, family emergency, etc.), please contact us as soon as possible. We will work with you to reschedule the session.

If I need to cancel or reschedule a session:

Refund Process

  1. Contact us via email or WhatsApp with your order details
  2. Provide your payment confirmation and reason for refund request
  3. We will review your request within 2-3 business days
  4. If approved, refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Refund Method

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method

Approved refunds will be:
